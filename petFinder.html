About US

Our Mission Statement

Every individual within our community matters, including our furry friends! Our mission is to provide the community with a light-hearted and unique game to connect members to a local animal adoption center. We hope that our website can deliver a fresh opportunity for each animal to be adopted into a loving home within their community, where they can finally recieve the care that they deserve.
